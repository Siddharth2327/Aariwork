Our Story

A Legacy Crafted
With Passion

For over 15 years, we have been weaving dreams into fabric — thread by thread, stitch by stitch. Rooted in the rich embroidery traditions of Tamil Nadu, our atelier in Tiruvannamalai has grown from a humble workshop into one of the region's most trusted names in bridal Aari embroidery.

Every blouse that leaves our hands carries the mark of patience and perfection. We serve brides not just as clients, but as collaborators — helping each one realise her unique vision for one of life's most important days.

2009
Founded

Began with a single needle, a vision, and a passion for the art of Aari embroidery.

2013
Expanded Services

Added Maggam work, Zardosi, and stone work to our growing catalogue.

2018
Bridal Specialist

Became the go-to atelier for bridal collections across Tiruvannamalai district.

2025
Trusted by Thousands

Over 5,000 happy customers and 10,000+ custom designs later — the journey continues.
